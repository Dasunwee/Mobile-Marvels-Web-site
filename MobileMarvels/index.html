Apple’s iPhone 16 launch event to be held on 9th September: Here’s what to expect.

Apple has just announced the date and time it will be holding its upcoming 2024 September event. Along with the announcement poster is a tagline that reads “It’s Glowtime”. Could this be an indication towards the new “glowing Siri” in Apple Intelligence?

The iPhone 16 lineup is most likely to be introduced during this event, featuring the iPhone 16, iPhone 16 Plus, iPhone 16 Pro, and the iPhone 16 Pro Max. The non-pro iPhone 16 and iPhone 16 Plus are expected to feature a new vertically aligned camera layout at the back, at least based on photos of dummy units that have been shared online.
